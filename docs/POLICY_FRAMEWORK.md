# Global Ethical AI Policy Framework
**UN/ILO Submission 2026**
**Author:** Ervin Remus Radosavlevici (ervin210@icloud.com)
**Status:** Production-Ready

---
## Preamble
This **binding global treaty** ensures AI and robotics **augment—not replace—human labor**, with:
1. **One-to-One Job Transition Guarantee**
2. **Automation Tax** funding reskilling
3. **Transnational Oversight Board (TAEB)**

---
## Article 1: Job Transition Mandate
- **Reassignment:** Workers displaced by automation get comparable/superior roles.
- **Retraining:** Fully funded upskilling (e.g., AI collaboration, robotics maintenance).
- **Income Guarantee:** Wage/benefit parity during transitions.

## Article 2: Global Governance
- **TAEB:** 50% worker representatives; audits corporations.
- **Transparency:** Corporations disclose automation plans 24 months in advance.

## Article 3: Economic Protections
- **Automation Tax:** 1–3% on corporate AI profits, funding:
  - Universal reskilling
  - Social safety nets (UBI pilots)
- **Profit Redistribution:** 20% of automation gains reinvested in workers.

## Article 4: Corporate Compliance
- **NDA Required:** Corporations must sign [NDA](../legal/NDA.md).
- **Penalties:** Fines up to 5% of revenue; criminal liability for executives.
